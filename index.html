How the Referral Program Works

Discover's referral program is designed to reward both you and your friends. Here's a detailed explanation:

  • As a current Discover cardholder, you can refer friends to apply for a Discover card.
  • If your friend is approved and makes their first purchase within 3 months, both you and your friend will receive a $100 bonus.
  • The bonus will be added to your cash back balance and can be redeemed like any other cash back rewards.

Steps to Participate in the Referral Program

  1. Log in to your Discover account online or through the mobile app.
  2. Navigate to the referral program section.
  3. Generate your unique referral link.
  4. Share your referral link with friends and family.
  5. Encourage your friends to apply using your link and make their first purchase within 3 months of approval.

Tips for Successfully Inviting Friends

Rules and Limitations of the Referral Program

It's important to be aware of the following points:

  • The referral bonus is only available for new cardmembers. Existing or previous Discover cardmembers are not eligible.
  • Both you and your friend must have an open account in good standing when the bonus is applied.
  • There may be a limit to the number of referral bonuses you can earn in a calendar year. Check the current terms for specific details.
  • The referral program is subject to change or termination at Discover's discretion.
